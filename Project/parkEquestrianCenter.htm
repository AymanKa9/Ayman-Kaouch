Park Equestrian Center

The Park Equestrian Center is a full-service facility located in the scenic hills of the countryside. We specialize in providing a safe, professional, and fun environment for both horses and riders.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality care for our horses and the best possible instruction for our riders. We believe that the bond between a human and a horse is a unique and rewarding experience that should be accessible to everyone.

Services Offered

  • Boarding and Stall Rentals
  • Riding Lessons (Beginner to Advanced)
  • Horse Training and Exercise
  • Summer Youth Programs
